Company Overview

White-label B2B SEO and digital marketing fulfillment partner exclusively serving digital marketing agencies — founded over 10 years ago and scaled to $4.65M in annual revenue with a 24.4% Adjusted EBITDA margin, more than double the industry benchmark for agencies at this stage.

The business operates as a behind-the-scenes fulfillment engine: agency partners place orders through a WooCommerce-based ecommerce platform, and the team handles everything from Google Business Profile management to link building to press distribution to full managed search campaigns.

5

Company Overview (cont'd)

Revenue is collected at point of sale via Stripe, PayPal, Payoneer, and Wise — eliminating accounts receivable risk entirely. The model combines recurring subscription orders with project-based purchases, creating a stable and predictable revenue foundation with built-in growth potential.

The five core service lines — Google Business Profile Management (largest team, 17 people), Link Acquisition & Building (major revenue driver), Press Syndication & PR Distribution, On-Page SEO & Technical Optimization, and Managed Search Campaigns (highest-value upsell) — provide diversification across both delivery and revenue streams.

6

Company Overview (cont'd)

Operations run on a technology stack of 100+ software tools ($300K+ annual spend), including Ahrefs, SEMRush, GoHighLevel, Active Campaign, and custom automation workflows. Press distribution networks (Newsfile Media, Comtex, XPR Media) and Meta advertising ($120K+/yr) drive both client acquisition and service delivery.

The team structure includes 8 US-based leadership positions managing specialized divisions: GBP (17), Signals & Links (9), Signal Builders (19), Accounts Builders (30), SEO & Managed Search (6), On-Page (6), Press (8), Marketing & Sales (3), and Design & Development (4). Every division operates under its own team lead with established processes.
